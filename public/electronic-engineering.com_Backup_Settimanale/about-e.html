Electronic Engineering started operations on 1991 as all brands service and repair center for musical instruments, professional audio gears, lighting and special effects.

Based on a many years working experience and on a growing technology, the Center successfully works on analogic and digital recorders, keyboards and workstations as on vintage gears (keyboards, organs and tube gears).

A good laboratory instrumentation and a direct contact with tech support people of brands, cuts the repair time at the minimum.

Our "all by computer" philosophy manages repair statistics and spare parts.
